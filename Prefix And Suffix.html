Prefix And Suffix

  • Prefix is a word which is added before a word to modify it’s meaning.
  • Suffix is a word which is added after a word to modify it’s meaning.
  • Sometimes, while adding suffixes we need to modify the word to get the correct spelling.

    • Examples:

    Prefix:

  • ___cover
  • ___like
  • __equal

    • Suffix:

  • Comfort____
  • Construct___
  • Success___

    • ANSWERS:

    Prefix:

  • DIScover
  • DISlike
  • UNequal

    • Suffix:

  • ComfortABLE
  • ConstructION
  • SuccessER
